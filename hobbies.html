Exercise and the Keto diet


Robert Egge’s workout routine contains several types of exercises, but the majority of them are body-weight training. Body-weight training exercises, as the name suggests, are exercises that use the practitioner’s weight as resistance to gain strength. Famous examples include pushups, planks, squats, situps, lunges, and pullups. One of the major benefits of body-weight training is that it requires no equipment an can be performed in most locations, though it is recommended that the practitioner does not over-exert themselves and that they maintain proper form.


Egge has been on a keto diet for several years now and mainly eats meat. A ketogenic dietis a high fat, low carb diet. While the diet is not for everyone, it drastically increases the body’s ability to process fats when it works. These fats are turned to ketones when they pass through the liver and are used as a brain-fuel alternative to sugars. As a result, blood sugar and insulin are significantly reduced.
